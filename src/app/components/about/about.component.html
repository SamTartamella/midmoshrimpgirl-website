About Me

I'm Sam and I love shrimp! My interest in aquariums began when YouTube autoplay started a video by Sherpa Design. From there, I started watching other aquarist channels and was always most charmed when little shrimp hands were chowing down on biofilm. How cute! I knew one day I had to have my own, and now here we are! I currently raise Neocardinia Shrimp and own lines of Cherry and Orange Rili. I hope to add more to my collection soon!
